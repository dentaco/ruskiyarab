The House Pour

Anatomy of a Negroni

01

The glass

A heavy rocks glass, chilled. The negroni deserves weight in your hand.

02

Ice

One big, clear cube. Slow dilution — the drink should evolve, not drown.

03

30ml gin

London dry. The backbone — juniper cutting through everything sweet.

04

30ml Campari

The bitter red soul of the drink. No substitutes, no apologies.

05

30ml sweet vermouth

Rosso. It rounds the bitterness into something you keep coming back to.

06

Stir & peel

20–30 seconds with a bar spoon. Express an orange peel over the top. Done.
